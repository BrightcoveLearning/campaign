Overview of Video Content Types

In this topic you will learn about the Video Content Types that are available.

By default, Brightcove Campaign can be enabled so that videos will have an additional metadata field, Video Content Type.

Available Video Content Types

The table below outlines the Video Content Types that are available inside Brightcove Campaign.

Video Content Type Description
Brand/Company Promotion Non-product specific videos that describe a company’s mission or values.  They often have high production values.
Product Overview Videos that focus on a specific product and how it benefits a consumer/user. Assume that the viewers have not used the product previously. These videos don’t delve into how to configure or operate a product but show the benefit in broad terms.
Product Details and Use Videos that show the viewer how to accomplish specific tasks within a product. These will include configurations or step-by-step instructions to accomplish a task. 
Testimonial Testimonials include non-employees discussing the benefits of engaging with a product or service or company. 
Event Promotion Videos that promote a conference, meetup, or online gathering. Videos that recap what happened at an event.
Company Culture Attempts to showcase the people and values of a company. These will include on-site footage and interviews with employees.
Webinar Online meeting in which a host presents a discussion on a topic that usually includes an opportunity for feedback from the audience.

Interviews/Presentations
Videos include a speaker delivering expert advice or research as part of thought leadership efforts.
