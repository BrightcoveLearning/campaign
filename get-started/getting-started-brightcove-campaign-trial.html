Getting Started with your Brightcove Campaign Trial

In this topic you will learn how to get started with a Brightcove Campaign trial account.

Brightcove Campaign is designed for demand generation marketers and makes it easy to configure, publish, and measure video performance within campaigns.

Trial limitations

Brightcove Campaign trial accounts have the following limitations:

  • A maximum of five videos can be uploaded to an account. If you need to be able to upload more than 5 videos while the account is in trial mode, you can contact our sales team and they will provide you with unrestricted access for uploading.
  • Media sharing is not available
  • Video poster images cannot be changed
